Platform Guide

Important: Activation requires a minimum trading balance of 400 USDT.

How activation works

When trading is activated, the current trading balance is locked as the cycle amount for exactly 5 minutes. After the cycle ends, a fixed profit of 7% is added to the trading account and activation is switched off automatically.

Transfer rules

  • Sender: Bot Account only
  • Receivers: Trading Account or Contract Account
  • Activation state: Saved for the user and restored after refresh until the 5-minute cycle ends

Saved records

  • Activation Log: Activation, manual stop and completed cycle are all stored in Firebase and shown permanently
  • My Status: Currency, date, amount, fixed profit 7%, and green completed status
